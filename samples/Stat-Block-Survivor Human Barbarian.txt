Twisted Flesh (Ex)
Surviving the chaos has left this barbarian more resilient, but hideously deformed. they gain a +1 natural armor bonus, a +1 bonus on Intimidate checks, and cold, electricity, and fire resistance 2. They also gain a –1 penalty on all other Charisma-based skills, Charisma checks, and Fortitude saves.
